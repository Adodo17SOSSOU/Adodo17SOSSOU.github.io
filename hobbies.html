Hobbies & other interests

Music

I enjoy playing the trumpet. Music has been a way for me to balance the intensity of studies and research, and to stay connected with others beyond the academic world.

Travel & cultures

Living in Togo, Morocco and France has made me very curious about languages and cultures. I like discovering new places, meeting people from different backgrounds and learning how education systems work in different countries.

Education & mentoring

Through Excellence Scholars, I regularly share information about scholarships and study abroad programmes, and try to support students who are applying for them. I am particularly interested in making opportunities in AI and data science more accessible for students from under-represented regions.

AI innovation & impact

Beyond pure technical work, I am interested in how AI can be used responsibly in healthcare, and how we can build tools that are both scientifically sound and socially beneficial.
